TEXAS STATE DISCLOSURES

Vehicle Service Contract

IMPORTANT NOTICE TO TEXAS RESIDENTS

This is not an insurance policy. This is a vehicle service contract that provides coverage for mechanical breakdown of covered components.

RIGHT TO CANCEL

You have the right to cancel this contract within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. After 30 days, cancellation is subject to a pro-rated refund minus administrative fees not to exceed $75.

COVERAGE LIMITATIONS

This contract does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Pre-existing conditions
  • Damage from accidents or collision
  • Damage from flood, fire, or other acts of nature
  • Maintenance items
  • Damage due to abuse or misuse

CLAIMS PROCEDURE

To file a claim:

  1. Contact the administrator immediately
  2. Do not authorize repairs without prior approval
  3. Use only authorized service facilities
  4. Submit all required documentation

DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this contract may be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

TEXAS SPECIFIC PROVISIONS

This contract complies with Texas Insurance Code Chapter 1304, relating to vehicle protection product warranties.
