1. COVERAGE TERMS

This Vehicle Service Contract provides coverage for mechanical breakdown of covered components as specified herein. Coverage begins on the effective date and continues for the term specified in the contract.

2. COVERED COMPONENTS

The following components are covered under this contract:

  • Engine and internal components
  • Transmission and internal components
  • Drive axle and internal components
  • Electrical systems
  • Air conditioning and heating systems
  • Steering and suspension systems
  • Brake systems

3. EXCLUSIONS

The following are not covered under this contract:

  • Normal wear and tear items
  • Damage due to abuse, misuse, or neglect
  • Pre-existing conditions
  • Damage from accidents or collision
  • Damage from flood, fire, or other acts of nature
  • Maintenance items such as oil changes, filters, and fluids

4. CLAIMS PROCESS

To file a claim, contact the administrator at the phone number provided. Claims must be reported within 30 days of the breakdown. Repairs must be performed by an authorized service facility.

5. CANCELLATION

This contract may be cancelled by the contract holder within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. After 30 days, cancellation is subject to a pro-rated refund minus administrative fees.

6. TRANSFERABILITY

This contract is transferable to a new owner upon sale of the vehicle, subject to transfer fees and administrative requirements.

This contract is governed by the laws of the state where the contract was purchased and is subject to the jurisdiction of the courts of that state.
